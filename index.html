Background
Rumah Anak Yatim dan Miskin Nur Iman

NUR IMAN Orphanage was founded by Ustaz Badrul Huzaini bin Mohd Yusof on December 20, 2010 with the help of friends who are committed to charity work. Managed by Nur lman Orphan and Poor Welfare Organization, a registered NGO.

NUR IMAN makes every effort to provide learning alternatives for orphans and poor students who do not excel in academics following religious studies (pondok) so that their lives are filled with activities that can build pious people, God willing. For students who excel in academic learning, NUR IMAN strives to guide them to have a solid understanding of religion in order to form a generation of orphans who excel academically and excel in agarna appreciation. NUR IMAN hopes that with this little effort, the lives of some orphans and poor children who are neglected from spiritual fulfillment will be saved. God willing.
